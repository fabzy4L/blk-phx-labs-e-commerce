## Verdicts

### Full Phoenix + Atom — `IMG_5568` series
**SELECTED MASTER MARK**

The full-body silhouette reads clearly at any size. The atom embedded in the chest is the right signal for the "science-backed" positioning. The navy background mockup (`IMG_5568(2).jpg`) has a premium near-metallic feel. This is the mark for the label, storefront, and all primary brand touchpoints.

- Action: Replace `1.png` on the Supliful label with this mark before submission.
- Affinity source: `IMG_5568(1).af` — edit here for label export.

---

### BLK·PHX LLC Flying Head — `303B4C7F`, `F424F36B`, `46B1D3A1`, `9B248098`
**Hold — needs cleanup**

Strong aggressive energy. The profile is bold and distinctive — good candidate for apparel, merch, or secondary brand marks. Not the lab/supplement primary mark.

- Blocker: AI text artifacts in the wordmark on at least two renders (garbled characters below "LLC"). Must be cleaned up or re-generated before any use.
- Do not use in any customer-facing context until artifact issue is resolved.

---

### Chrome / Neon AI Renders — `IMG_5737`, `IMG_5738`, `IMG_5739`
**Digital only — not print-safe**

Visually impressive on screen. At label size (≤1" height) the texture and glow collapse into noise. Not usable for Supliful submission or any print application.

- Use case: social media content, digital ads, brand mood board only.

---

### Geometric Origami Phoenix — `1.png`
**Superseded**

Currently on the label. Clean and technically precise but reads as rougher and less premium compared to the full-body silhouette. Replace with the `IMG_5568` master mark before Supliful submission.

- Do not use as the primary mark going forward.
- May still work as a secondary geometric utility mark if needed.

---

### Minimal White-on-Grey — `IMG_5229`
**Utility mark only**

Useful at very small sizes: favicon, embossing, watermark, packaging stamp. Not a primary brand logo.
